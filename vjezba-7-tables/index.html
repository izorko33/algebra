Emergency Dentist

Based in Leeds City Centre on Wormald Row, and open 24 hours a day, we provide evening, weekend and late night dental appointments to patients in Leeds and the surrounding areas. You do not need to be registered with us in order to be seen. If you are in pain, or would like to be seen urgently we offer same day appointments and will usually be able to offer treatment at the same appointment. We understand that many patients will be nervous when visiting the dentist, particularly for an emergency, and it may have been a number of years since you were last seen. No matter what the problem, we will do our best to put you at ease and make your experience as relaxed as possible.
